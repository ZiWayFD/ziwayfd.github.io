The Problem with Single-System Readings

Every divination system works. The problem is: each one only sees part of you.

BaZi says you're driven and ambitious. Numerology says you're a natural healer. Which is right? Both — but you'd never know from either one alone.

Most readers pick one system and stick with it. That's like diagnosing a patient by only looking at their blood work and ignoring the MRI. You get a reading. You don't get the picture.

Why Five Dimensions?
